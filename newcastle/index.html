Newcastle City Council Spending

An ODI Leeds project to visualise Newcastle City Council spending since April 2010. Between 2010 and October 2012 this only included expenditure over £500. Since October 2014 Newcastle City Council have published all transactions over £250 (except for payments made to staff, housing benefit payments, sensitive social services information which could put vulnerable individuals at risk, personal information, confidential information). This is a work-in-progress but let us know if it is useful.
